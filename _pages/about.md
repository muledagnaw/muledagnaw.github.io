My name is Dr. Mulugeta Alemayehu Dagnaw. I am an assistant Professor of Mathematics at Debre Tabor University. I have taught undergraduate mathematics courses throughout my teaching career. My research is on Clifford analysis, Mathematics education, Partial differential equations (PDEs) and Ordinary differential equations (ODEs). Lately my interest focuses in Boundary-domain integral equations.
