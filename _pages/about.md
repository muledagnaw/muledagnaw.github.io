My name is Mulugeta Alemayehu Dagnaw (PhD). I am an assistant Professor of Mathematics at Debre Tabor University. I have taught undergraduate mathematics courses throughout my teaching career. My research is on Clifford analysis, Mathematics education and Partial differential equations (PDEs). Currently my interest focuses in Boundary-domain integral equations.
