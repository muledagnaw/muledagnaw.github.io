My name is Mulugeta Alemayehu Dagnaw. I am a Lecturer  of Mathematics at Debre Tabor University. I have taught undergraduate mathematics courses throughout my teaching career. My research is on Clifford analysis, Mathematics education, Partial differential equations(PDEs) and Ordinary differential equations(ODEs). Lately my interest focuses in Boundary-domain integral equations.
